Creatures

Creautres are a type of card. You can 'cast' a creautre card for its mana cost to summon it on to the board, and it will stay on the board until its destroyed. Creature cards have a "Power" and "Toughness". Power determines how much damage a creature deals to players and other creatures, and toughness determines how much damage a creature can take before its destroyed. Some creature cards can also have abilities that affect how a creature battles, or interacts with other cards. Creautre cards also have a type, such as 'Bird', 'Human', 'Dragon', and many others. Using creautres to attack your opponent is the most straightforward method to winning a game.
