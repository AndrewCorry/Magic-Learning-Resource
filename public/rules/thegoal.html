The Goal

Magic: The Gathering is a tabletop card game where you play a wizard equiped with a selection of powerful spells to cast and creatures to summon. These spells and creatures are represented by the cards in your deck, and you can choose whatever cards you want in that deck. Each player starts the game with 20 'Life' and a deck of at least 60 cards. The goal of every game of Magic is to reduce your opponent's Life to 0 using spells and creatures before they can do the same to you.
