Example Cards

White

White is the color of order and morality. White is represented by a symbol of the sun, and its lands are Plains. White is a defensive color, and specializes in controlling battles with blockers and damage negation. Its creatures have abilities such as Vigilance, which allows a creature to attack without tapping, and Lifelink, which gives the player Life points equal to the damage they deal. The signature creature types of White are Humans, Angels, and Unicorns. White spells can prevent your opponets creatures from attacking or dealing damage, can increase your life points, and can even remove cards your opponent is using from the game by "exiling" them. Because of its defensive nature, White is a slow color offensively. White cards can stop your opponent's offense, but it can be difficult to start your own.
