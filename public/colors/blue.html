Blue

Blue is the color of knowledge and curiosity. Blue is represented by a symbol of water, and its lands are Islands. Blue is a tricky color, and focuses more on preventing its opponent's offense or defense rather than bolstering its own. Blue's creatures are weaker on average, but have strong abilities, such as being able to send cards back to your opponent's hand, or copy your opponent's creatures. Signature creature types of Blue are Birds, Merfolk, and Shapeshifters. Blue is host to among the most poweful spells in Magic. Blue spells can draw you more cards, can "counterspell" your oppoent's cards, which prevent them from being cast in the first place, and even let you take control of your opponent's cards. Blue's spells give it an edge over other colors, but its creatures are liable to lose in a battle.
