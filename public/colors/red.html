Example Cards

Red

Red is the color of freedom and emotion. Red is represented by a symbol of flame, and its lands are Mountains. Red is an aggressive color, and has straightforward spells and creatures. Many of its cards cost a small amount of mana, and are hard to defend against. Red creatures have an emphasis of speed and Power over Toughness. The signature creature types of Red are Goblins, Minotaurs, and Dragons. They have abilites such as Haste, which lets creatures attack the turn they are summoned, and First Strike, which lets creatures attack before blockers. Red spells are defined by the ability to deal direct damage to creatures or players, or cause their creatures to deal more damage. Although Red cards are strong early in a match, they lose to the higher cost cards of other colors. If Red doesn't win fast, it probably won't win at all.
