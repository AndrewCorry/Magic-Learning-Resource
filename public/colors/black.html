Example Cards

Black

Black is the color of resourcefulness and amorality. Its represented by a symbol of a skull, and its lands are Swamps. Black is a balanced color, capable of mounting an effective offense and defense. Black cards are often double edged swords, granting powerful effects at the cost of life or your own creatures in addition to mana. Black creatures can cause your opponent's creatures to become permenantly weaker, can instantly kill creatures they battle with, and can even bring themselves back after being destroyed. Signature Black creature types are Zombies, Vampires, and Demons. Black spells can cause your opponent to discard cards, can search your deck and draw a specific card, and can destory your opponent's cards outright. Black cards are overall strong, but because of their additional costs of life or creautres they can be difficult to use.
